## Image Specifications:

### Required Sizes:
1. **Hero Image**: 1920x1080px (16:9 landscape)
2. **Category Images**: 600x800px (3:4 portrait)
3. **Product Images**: 800x800px (1:1 square)
4. **Offer Banners**: 1200x800px (3:2 landscape)
5. **Lookbook Image**: 1920x1080px (16:9 landscape)
6. **Instagram Posts**: 600x600px (1:1 square)
7. **Testimonial Avatars** (optional): 150x150px (1:1 square)

### Image Format:
- **Recommended**: JPEG or WebP
- **Quality**: High quality, optimized for web
- **Content**: All images should be Indian traditional saree photos

### Total Count:
- **Minimum Required**: 25 images
- **With Testimonials**: 28 images
